Everything You Need to Know

Answers to the most common questions about partnering with Abstrakt for outsourced sales development.

Abstrakt is an outsourced sales development partner that owns the entire revenue cycle for commercial service companies. We handle everything from identifying your ideal prospects and executing multi-channel outreach to booking qualified appointments and helping close deals. Think of us as your complete, dedicated outbound sales team—without the overhead of building one internally.

Lead gen companies hand you a list of names and wish you luck. Abstrakt is fundamentally different—we own the entire revenue cycle from first touch to closed deal. We don't just generate leads; we prospect, nurture, qualify, book appointments, and support your team through to closed-won revenue. We're not a vendor; we're your outsourced sales department.

We specialize in 13 commercial service industries: HVAC, Roofing, Construction, Landscaping, Paving, Commercial Cleaning, Flooring, Electrical, Fire Protection, Painting, Solar, General Contracting, and Plumbing. Our deep specialization means dedicated teams with refined talk tracks, buyer intelligence, and proven sales strategies built specifically for each industry.

Our BDR-as-a-Service model gives you a dedicated Business Development Representative team that works exclusively on your account. They build your target market using our 125M+ contact database, execute multi-channel outreach (cold calls, email sequences, LinkedIn, direct mail), qualify prospects on budget, timeline, and authority, and deliver verified decision-maker meetings directly to your calendar. It's a fully managed outbound sales engine.

A typical Abstrakt partnership includes: dedicated BDR team, target market build and ICP development, multi-channel outreach execution, lead nurturing and follow-up, qualified appointment setting, CRM integration and reporting, creative services (sales collateral, email templates), exclusive territory protection, and ongoing strategy optimization. Everything you need to fill your pipeline without building an internal team.

Outbound BDR programs typically range from ~$5,000 to $10,000 per month depending on scope, territory size, and services included. This is significantly less than the fully loaded cost of hiring even one internal SDR (salary, benefits, tools, training, management). We'll build a custom proposal based on your industry, geography, and growth goals. Contact us for a detailed quote.

Our standard partnership agreements are structured to give both sides enough time to build momentum and see real results. No tire-kickers, no gatekeepers—just real opportunities.
