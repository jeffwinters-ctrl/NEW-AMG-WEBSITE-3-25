The 6-Step Process

Your Path to Predictable Revenue

Each step is owned by Abstrakt. You stay focused on running your business while we build, manage, and close your pipeline.

1. Discovery & ICP Build

We start by deeply understanding your business—your ideal customer profile, competitive advantages, service area, and revenue goals. We then build a comprehensive Ideal Customer Profile (ICP) that targets the exact decision-makers who control buying in your market: VPs of Facilities, Property Managers, Directors of Operations, and more. This foundation ensures every outreach effort is precisely aimed at your highest-value prospects.

2. Market Research & List Building

Using our database of 125M+ records combined with proprietary research, we build a verified, exclusive target list for your territory. Every contact is researched, enriched, and validated—not scraped from a generic database. We identify companies by size, industry, location, and buying signals so your BDR team is reaching out to prospects who are ready to have a conversation about your services.

3. Multi-Channel Launch

Your dedicated BDR team launches a coordinated multi-channel campaign across cold calls, personalized email sequences, LinkedIn outreach, and direct mail. Every touchpoint is crafted using talk tracks and messaging refined across ~1,000 commercial service clients. This isn't spray-and-pray—it's a strategic cadence designed to break through to busy decision-makers and start real sales conversations.

4. Qualification & Nurturing

Not every prospect is ready to buy today. Our team qualifies every lead on budget, authority, timeline, and need (BANT). Prospects who aren't ready get placed into nurture sequences that keep your company top-of-mind until the timing is right. Prospects who are ready get fast-tracked to a meeting. Nothing falls through the cracks—every contact is accounted for and actively worked.

5. Meeting Setting & Handoff

When a prospect is qualified, your BDR sets a confirmed meeting with all relevant details: company info, decision-maker name and role, pain points discussed, budget range, and timeline. The meeting lands on your calendar with a full briefing so your sales team walks in prepared. No cold introductions—every meeting is warm, qualified, and ready for your pitch.

6. Close Support & Reporting

We don't hand off and disappear. Abstrakt provides ongoing close support, following up with prospects, nurturing stalled deals, and providing weekly pipeline reports with full visibility into activity metrics, conversion rates, and revenue attribution. We stay in the deal until it's closed won—and then we keep filling the top of your funnel for sustained growth.
